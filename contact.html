Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my passwords stored?

Your passwords are stored locally in your browser's secure storage. They never leave your device and are encrypted before storage. No data is transmitted to any servers.

How does PassVault work?

PassVault generates strong passwords and stores them locally in your browser. Your data stays on your device for easy access and convenience.

Can I sync passwords between devices?

Currently, PassVault stores passwords locally. However, you can export your passwords as a JSON file and import them on other devices for manual syncing.

Is PassVault free to use?

Yes, PassVault is completely free and open-source. We believe strong security should be accessible to everyone without cost.
